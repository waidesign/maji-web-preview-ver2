Signature Therapy

The world's smallest molecule.
The body's deepest repair.

Molecular hydrogen (H₂) measures just 0.289 nanometres — small enough to penetrate every cell membrane, cross the blood-brain barrier, and enter mitochondria directly. At Maji, we deliver it through two complementary pathways: transdermal immersion via the stone hydrogen bath, and pulmonary absorption via hydrogen inhalation therapy.

Unlike crude antioxidant supplements, H₂ selectively neutralises only the most destructive free radicals — the hydroxyl radical (·OH) and peroxynitrite (ONOO⁻) — leaving beneficial reactive oxygen species intact to support normal cellular signalling.

The Protocol

Three stages.
One complete physiological reset.

The Maji thermal journey follows a precise biological logic. First, heat — drawn from Russian Banya, Finnish Sauna, or Hammam — elevates core temperature, opens vascular channels, and primes the body's detoxification mechanisms. Cold immediately follows: the plunge pool constricts vessels, drives circulation deep, and activates cold-shock proteins that amplify cellular repair. The final stage — hydrogen bath or inhalation — delivers molecular restoration at the most fundamental level.
